How frameworks are selected

The Schools Commercial Team (SCT) within DfE reviews a range of frameworks. These frameworks are assessed for:

  • value for money
  • compliance with procurement regulations
  • ease of use
  • suitability for schools

Feedback from schools is also considered when selecting frameworks.

SCT continues to review and add to the list of recommended frameworks.
