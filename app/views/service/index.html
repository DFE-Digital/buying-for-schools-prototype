From: Department for Education

{{ serviceName }}.

This service replaces the deals for schools guidance.

If you’re a school buyer, use this service to:

  • find Department for Education (DfE) recommended ways to buy goods or services
  • get value for money
  • comply with EU procurement law (OJEU)

How are frameworks selected?

The Schools Commercial Team (SCT) within DfE reviews a range of frameworks. These frameworks are assessed for compliance with procurement regulations, ease of use, suitability and value for money. Feedback from schools is also considered when selecting frameworks.

SCT continues to review and add to the list of recommended frameworks.

What happens after you’ve used the service?

After you’ve answered the relevant questions, you’ll be given the most suitable framework based on your answers.
