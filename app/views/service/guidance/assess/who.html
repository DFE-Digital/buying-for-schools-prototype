Fairly assess all bids

Who should assess the bids

It’s best to have at least two people assess each bid. The more people involved, the harder it can be to reach an agreed score.

When they’re finished they should compare their scores and:

  • discuss where they’ve scored differently
  • reach an agreed score

Bring in a specialist if you need expert knowledge to assess a criteria.
