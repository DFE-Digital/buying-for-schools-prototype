How to buy from a framework

  • 1

    Prepare an invite to tender

    Invitations should include information like your timeline, specifcation, and your award criteria.

  • 2

    Send your invitation to the suppliers

    You need to send it to all suppliers who can provide what you need, unless you run an expression of interest process first.

  • 3

    Fairly assess all the bids you get

    Choose the supplier that offers best value for money, assessing them using the award criteria you sent out in your invitation to tender.

  • 4

    Award the contract to the winning supplier

    You need to inform all of the suppliers, and then your school or organisation and the supplier should sign the contract.
