Your specification

Your specification is what you give to suppliers explaining what you want to buy.

Your specification should include:

  • a precise description of what you need
  • an explanation of what it should do, if needed
  • the amount you need
  • what quality it should be
  • when you need it

Think about what is essential, what would be nice to have and what you’ll need in 1 or 2 year’s time.

Check you’ve included everything you need. If you miss something out it may cost more once you have a supplier, as there will be nobody left competing to supply it cheaper.
