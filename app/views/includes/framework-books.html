Your recommendation

Books and related materials

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Eastern Shires Purchasing Organisation (ESPO) framework.

What it offers

  • books
  • e-books
  • e-audiobooks
  • educational software
  • CDs
  • DVDs

Includes fiction, non-fiction and curriculum material.

Benefits

  • discounts of over 40% on some titles

Before buying from the framework you’ll need to:

  • create an ESPO account
  • follow the instructions in the ESPO guide

How to use the framework

Create a MyESPO account

To access discounts, you need to create a MyESPO account.

Creating an account is normally very quick – we can usually set up your account on the same day. You’ll receive an email when we activated your account.

Create your ESPO account.

If you already have a MyESPO account

You may already have an account with ESPO if you’ve used the ESPO catalogue before.

However, to access the frameworks, you need another account that lets you order online at www.espocatalogue.org. This does not necessarily mean you already have an account to view frameworks on www.espo.org – you may have one or the other, both or neither.

You can register to access frameworks whether your school has a catalogue account with us or not. If you’re not sure which option to choose, select ‘I am a new customer’. This will let you progress whatever your current situation is.

If you’re shown the message ‘This email address has been used in a previous application’, then you already have a framework account and will be able to log in. If you are having difficulties logging in, select the ‘Forgotten your password’ option.

Use the framework

Log on to your MyESPO account and go to the framework page.

You can find all forms in the ‘downloads’ section of this framework, including the user guide containing supplier details, discount information and further details about using the framework.

Complete the Customer Access Agreement form

You must complete the Customer Access Agreement form to get access to exclusive ESPO discounts.

By signing this form, you agree to use the framework in line with the process. You can order books once we have processed your form.

Please return completed forms by email to care@espo.org.

Find out suppliers’ discount rates

Rates are listed by the publisher to help you identify suppliers who offer the best discounts.

If you do not know the publisher of the books you need, you can:

  • search suppliers’ websites (see page 12 of the user guide)
  • search by book title or ISBN

Contact your chosen supplier

You should contact your chosen supplier to confirm:

  • availability
  • delivery times
  • pricing – the quote should reflect the discount from the recommended retail price (RRP)
  • stated within the user guide

You can find contact details for all suppliers on page 6 of the user guide.

Place your order with the chosen supplier

There are two ways you can place your order:

  • download and use the template order form provided
  • place your order by telephone or email

You must quote ‘ESPO Framework 376E’ to get the agreed discounts.

You should avoid using the suppliers’ order forms, These will contain the suppliers’ order terms and you may not get the best rates.

Run a further competition between the suppliers

You can run a further competition if you think you can get a better price.

This usually only applies to items ordered in large quantities.
