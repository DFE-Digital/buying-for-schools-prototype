Books and related materials

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Eastern Shires Purchasing Organisation (ESPO) framework.

What it offers

  • books
  • e-books
  • e-audiobooks
  • educational software
  • CDs
  • DVDs
  • discounts of over 40% on some titles

Includes fiction, non-fiction and curriculum material.

How to use the framework

Find out suppliers’ discount rates

Rates are listed by publishers on page 8 of the user guide to help you identify suppliers who offer the best discounts.

If you do not know the publisher of the books you need, visit any of the suppliers’ websites (page 12 of the user guide) and search by book title or ISBN.

Run a further competition between the suppliers

If you think you can get a better price, you can run a mini-competition. This usually only applies to items ordered in large quantities.

Contact your chosen supplier

You should contact your chosen supplier to confirm availability, delivery times and pricing – the quote should reflect the discount from the recommended retail price (RRP) stated within the user guide.

You can find contact details of all suppliers on page 6 of the user guide.

Place your order with the chosen supplier

There are two ways you can place your order:

  • using the template order form
  • by telephone or email

You must quote ‘ESPO Framework 376E’ to get the agreed discounts.
