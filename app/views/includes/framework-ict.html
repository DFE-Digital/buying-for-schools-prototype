ICT services for education

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Crown Commercial Services (CCS) framework.

What it offers

  • supply and fit
  • support and maintenance
  • systems configuration and management

  • complete integrated service with other technologies and partners
  • supply and integration of solutions into green field (new build) situations and legacy environments

Benefits

  • support with running mini-competitions
