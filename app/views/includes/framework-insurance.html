Risk protection arrangement (RPA) for academy trusts

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Department for Education (DfE) offering.

What it offers

An alternative to commercial insurance for academies that covers:

  • material damage
  • business interruption
  • employers’ liability
  • third party liability
  • governors’ liability
  • professional indemnity
  • employee and third party dishonesty
  • finance
  • personal accident
  • United Kingdom travel
  • overseas travel (including winter sports)
  • legal expenses
  • cultural assets
