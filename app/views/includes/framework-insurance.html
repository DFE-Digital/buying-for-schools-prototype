Risk protection arrangement for academy trusts

Based on your answers, we think you should use the Department for Education (DfE).

What we offer

  • an alternative to commercial insurance for academies that covers:
    • material damage
    • business interruption
    • employers liability
    • third party liability
    • governors’ liability
    • professional indemnity
    • employee and third party dishonesty
    • money
    • personal accident
    • United Kingdom travel
    • overseas travel including winter sports
    • legal expenses
    • cultural assets
  • free advice and training

Benefits

  • easy to opt-in
  • £20 per pupil taken direct from general annual grant
